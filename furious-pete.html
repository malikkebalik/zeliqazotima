Who is Furious Pete?

Peter Czerwinski is a Canadian-born bodybuilder and Guinness World Records holder who is better known under his nickname "Furious Pete. He has Polish decants, but most of his life and early childhood were spent in Toronto. Peter also has a YouTube channel and often competes in eating contests.

Peter was born on November 30, 1985 in Toronto, Canada. He is Canadian by nationality and Polish by ethnicity. The faith he strongly believes in is Christianity.
