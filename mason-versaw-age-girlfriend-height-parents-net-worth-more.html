Acting Journey With Education

Mason had a passion for performing arts since childhood; that’s why when he was studying at Lakeside High School, he began his acting journey as a theater artist by participating in High school plays where he performed in “Comic Book Artist” “The Wiz,” “Les Miserables,” and  “Guys and Dolls” theater acts and aside from school Plays Versaw also worked in the “Bird Kingdom,” and “West Side Story” at Ashtabula Art Center.

Although Mason began his acting career in high school, but he took acting and vocal classes at the “Ashtabula Art Center” to polish his abilities before enrolling at the Lee Strasberg Institute in New York, where he learned “Method Acting” and “Film/ Television” methods but after that, he didn’t stop learning and has taken “Young Adult” and “Sensory” and “Structured Drama Improv” Class by Sheila Grey.

On the one hand, Mason continuously worked on his skills; along with this, he began giving auditions; that’s why very soon, he got the chance to work in “Apple Music Holidays,” “Instagram Update,” and “Tub O Towels” Commercial, and by that he made his presence in the entertainment world.

Versaw never denied any opportunity in his journey; that’s why in 2019, he got the chance to work with famous American singer-songwriter Olivia O’Brien’s music Video “Fade Out,” and in the same year, he became the Finalist in “ABC Showcase.”

On the big screen, Mason got the chance to take his first step in 2021 by getting the chance to make his appearance as Donald Turner in “Blue Bloods” series one episode “The New Normal,” and you will be surprised to hear that this talented star made his landing in “Gossip Girl” & “tick, tick… BOOM!” in the same year.
