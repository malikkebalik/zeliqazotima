Maggie Lindemann: Career

Lindemann got the opportunity to be a singer when her manager Gerald Tennison discovered a video of her singing on Instagram. Soon the singer relocated to Los Angeles to pursue her music career further.

She then released her debut song, ‘Knocking On Your Heart,‘ in September 2015. Within just 24 hours, the song entered the Top 20 on the iTunes Alternative Chart. 

Influenced by the artist like Lana Del Rey, Banks, and Spooky Black, Lindemann too put her own flair to the songs unconcerned with being mainstream. Soon after, she released her second single, ‘Couple Of Kids,’ on October 30, 2015, followed by her third single, ‘Things,’ on January 29, 2016.

‘Things,’ upon its release, broke the Top 50 on Spotify and entered the charts worldwide, including number 5 in Canada. But the song ‘Pretty Girl,’ released on September 29, 2016, became even more successful by charting on the US Pop Radio Charts and number 8 on the UK Singles Chart. In fact, it was her first single to achieve such a feat.
