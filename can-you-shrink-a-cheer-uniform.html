Save the Heat for the Big Game Never put a sports uniform in the clothes dryer. High heat causes shrinking, sets in stains, and fades colors. If you are in a rush and must use a dryer, choose the air only cycle and allow the uniform to tumble for about ten minutes. Also question is, can you dry a cheer uniform? -Hang up uniform pieces to dry thoroughly using a plastic or molded hanger. Clothespins and wire hangers can make creases and misshape the fabric. -Don’t put your uniform in the clothes dryer. This will round out pleats and may leave bubbles from the heat.Can you wash cheer uniform?We recommend using Cheer, Tide Sport and, when necessary, a color-safe bleach. Wash your uniform alone and in cold water in the washing machine. It’s even better if you can wash your uniform by hand, but be careful not to wring it out too aggressively. The best practice is to let your uniform air-dry.
