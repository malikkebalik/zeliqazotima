'90 Day Fiance' Star Larissa Lima Threatens Legal Action Against Colt Johnson for Sharing Her Nude P

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  757 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Colt Johnson is the latest 90 Day Fiancé star to start an Onlyfans account. But after discovering he’s been using their pictures on his new page without consent, his exes, Larissa Dos Santos Lima and Jess Caroline, are shocked and angry. And in a recent post, Larissa claimed that Colt shared nude photos of her, and she’s threatening to take him to court. 

The ’90 Day Fiancé’ star has been accused of sending private photos before

In April 2020, after the current season 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After had finished filming, Jess accused Colt of sending her private photos to people without her consent. She said she received messages from a man that claimed to be Colt’s friend who showed her the pictures Colt sent him. 

“Hello everyone, I am really nervous right now about a DM I just received by a guy who says he is Colt Johnson’s friend, my ex-boyfriend, a guy who uses women to stay relevant on the 90 Day Fiance show,” Jess wrote on Instagram. “He is the only person who I trusted to sending private pictures while we were dating long distance.  Now I just got this DM from his friend and he told me Colt sent it to him.”

Larissa responded to Jess’ post soon after. And she hinted that the same may have been done to her. “Sorry sis to been through revenge porn [sic],” Larissa commented. “He abuses women in every way, he has [to] stop!”

Colt is using Jess and Larissa’s images on his Onlyfans page

Colt just started an Onlyfans account, and on his page, he is apparently using images of Larissa and Jess without consent. On Instagram, both of his exes slammed him and demanded he take them down. 

“This is what a passive-aggressive b*tch does,” Jess wrote in an Instagram story. “First he sent out my nudes, and now he is using my face on his ridiculous Onlyfans account. It calls gaslighting. By the way, who is going to pay to see a Vienna Sausage he sent out to thousands of chicks for free.”

Larissa made up a song asking why Colt is still so obsessed with her and Jessica. “Someone tell to the a**hole of my ex-husband to take my photo down off his damn Onlyfans account or secrets gonna be revealed,” she wrote in a follow-up Instagram post. “Trying to deal with everything legally but you keep bugging me with your f***ing life, get a life, idiot!”

The ’90 Day Fiancé’ star is threatening to take her ex-husband to court

In another Instagram story, Larissa asked her fans to suggest a good family law attorney in Las Vegas. She then posted a video revealing she needs the lawyer to take legal action against Colt for using her pictures without consent.

“I did not authorize him to use in any degree my photos or images on his Onlyfans accounts,” Larissa said. “And I don’t care if it’s a cartoon, I don’t care what it is. I just don’t want to be related to him.”

She then addressed fans criticizing her for talking about Colt on the show. The reality star noted that the season was filmed last year and that since, Colt has sent her nude pictures to his girlfriend, who then sent those pics to her own ex-husband.

“The show was filmed a while ago, and he sent my nudes to his girlfriend, that sent to her husband, so this is a felony here in the United States,” she said, noting her own attorney is now away on personal leave.

Larissa said that Colt’s girlfriend’s ex reached out to her and taunted her, showing her the nudes Colt sent out. The Brazilian native also suggested that Colt shared nudes of Jess. And she noted that both she and Jess would rather send money to their families in Brazil than spend it on legal fees suing Colt. 

“It’s not fair that we spend the money we could send to our families to sue this guy,” Larissa adds. “But the only way he’s going to stop is he be sued.”

“Okay, let’s go, let’s go by the court,” she addressed her ex.  “Explain why you had sent my nudes — Explain everything for the court. Stop, grow, be a man. Stop with this sh*t. Nobody care anymore. You got roasted, destroyed this season, Accept that you are a loser, and you’re gonna die a loser because you’re never gonna grow, you’re never gonna learn.”

Colt did not immediately respond to Larissa’s post.
