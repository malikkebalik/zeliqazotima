The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Thomas Comes Home Engaged to Someone Else Too Late for Hope

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  424 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers reveal that Thomas Forrester’s (Matthew Atkinson) time off-screen could open the door to a new love story. After Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) convinced Thomas to leave Hope and head for Paris with Douglas Forrester (Henry Joseph Samiri), they indeed hopped on a flight out of town.

Although Thomas wanted a future with Hope, she couldn’t give him the commitment he was looking for.

It was all or nothing for Thomas, so he bolted when Hope turned down his proposal for the second time.

It’s clear that Thomas is in a lot of pain despite his decision to walk away.

Thomas may be eager to fill that hole in his heart quickly, so what if he has a speedy romance in Paris and makes another fast proposal?

Thomas admitted he was hoping to find someone who could fully reciprocate his love, so maybe there’s really a woman out there who will.

Could Thomas meet some lovely lady and become smitten with her right off the bat?

Of course, we know that Ashleigh Brewer is reprising her role as Ivy Forrester for some new B&B appearances, so there’s been speculation that Thomas and Ivy might get together.

Since Thomas isn’t a Forrester by blood and has expressed romantic interest in Ivy in the past, never say never on them being a couple when Thomas comes back to town!

However, no one can rule out the idea of Thomas falling for someone new, especially if the show wants to add a fresh face to the canvas.

What if Hope eventually decides she’s willing to give Thomas the commitment he wants?

The next time Thomas visits Los Angeles with Douglas, Hope might ask for another chance and admit she’d say yes if Thomas asked her to marry him once more.

That’s when Thomas could reveal that he already asked someone else – and she said yes!

It’ll be a real twist if Thomas manages to put an engagement ring on another woman’s finger by the time he returns to LA.

Thomas might bring the new lady in his life home with him and show her off, so stick with us for updates on any engagement news that might be looming.

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers say Thomas’ love life could look a lot different soon, so we’ll bring you predictions on any Paris developments.
