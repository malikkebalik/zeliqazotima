General Hospital Spoilers: Week of June 19 Prison Plans, Stunning Revelations and Dangerous Dirt

 Posted on July 13, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  663 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

General Hospital (GH) spoilers for the week of June 19-23 reveal that Curtis Ashford (Donnell Turner) and Trina Robinson (Tabyana Ali) will get a chance to build on their bond. Trina and Curtis will get closer, so Portia Robinson (Brook Kerr) should be pleased to see them getting along so well.

Meanwhile, Jordan Ashford (Tanisha Harper) will get an interesting opportunity, so that might come through Zeke Robinson (Gavin Houston).

Since Jordan will get an unexpected visitor later, that could be Portia if she wants to steer the police commissioner away from her brother by then.

Curtis will eventually owe Portia an explanation, so the truth about his stolen kiss with Jordan may come to light and leave him scrambling to explain himself.

Over with Austin Gatlin-Holt (Roger Howarth), he’ll have a warning for Laura Collins (Genie Francis).

Ava Jerome (Maura West) may try to reason with Laura too, but her mind seems made up.

Despite all the attempts to talk Laura out of her Russia journey, she may stick to her plan and hope she can find Nikolas Cassadine (last played by Adam Huss).

Of course, Austin and Ava will continue to keep secrets based on what they think they know about Nikolas’ fate.

Mason Gatlin (Nathanyael Grey) will remain a dangerous threat, so Ava will face more pressure to do his dirty work and gather intel from Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard). That could certainly lead to bad news as this story moves along.

Over with Drew Cain (Cameron Mathison), he’ll have an important conversation with Sam McCall (Kelly Monaco) – presumably about his desire to strike a deal regarding the SEC mess.

Since it’ll affect Scout Cain (Cosette Abinante) if Drew goes to prison, that’s obviously something he needs to talk to Sam about.

At some point, Drew will face a blow that leaves him blindsided, but he’ll stand his ground.

That could mean Drew won’t get the lenient deal he has in mind, so he could be facing a far longer stint in the slammer than he anticipated.

Carly Spencer (Laura Wright) will end up furious, so we’ll see if that ties in with this Drew situation or something else.

Regardless, Nina Reeves (Cynthia Watros) will land in a predicament where she’s forced to think quickly.

If Nina starts to confess her role in turning Drew and Carly in for insider trading, she might back out of the admission once it becomes apparent that Ned Quartermaine (Wally Kurth) doesn’t remember.

The Quartermaine clan will react to some drama related to Ned’s awakening, so Olivia Quartermaine (Lisa LoCicero) will push for answers.

Ned will also need to see a specialist, so his memory issues could take center stage.

Tracy Quartermaine (Jane Elliot) will end up putting someone on full blast, so she may bark orders at GH and demand better care for Ned.

Later, Tracy will get into another argument with Gregory Chase (Gregory Harrison), so that’ll bring some insults and cold glares.

Other General Hospital spoilers say TJ Ashford (Tajh Bellow) will confide in Alexis Davis (Nancy Lee Grahn), so he may take a break from all the work chaos and share how Molly Lansing-Davis (temporarily played by Holiday Mia Kriegel) is coping with her infertility issues.

While Sasha Gilmore (Sofia Mattsson) faces a shocker, Cody Bell (Josh Bell) will push Gladys Corbin (Bonnie Burroughs) for a meeting that Selina Wu (Lydia Look) ultimately has to play peacemaker for.

Gladys will remain in hot water over the debt she owes, so things are about to blow up in her face soon enough.

Finally, Lucy Coe (Lynn Herring) will open up to Maxie Jones (Kirsten Storms), so we’ll see if that ties in with Deception, Lucy’s personal life or something else.

General Hospital spoilers say the week of June 19-23 will bring some curveballs, so stick with us for updates on all the action.
