New Holland TC29 Compact Utility Tractor is fitted with a Shibaura diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 81.2 ci [1.3 L] engine, which delivers 29 hp [21.6 kW] power at a rated rpm 2800.

New Holland TC29 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Shibaura 1.3L 3-cyl diesel engine, 9 forward and 3 reverse gears, 4×2 2WD/4×4 MFWD 4WD, hydrostatic power steering, wet disc brakes, and 1635 lbs [741 kg] Rear lift (at 24″/610mm).

New Holland TC29 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a 60″ Mower deck, 72″ Mower deck,  60″ Snow blower, Blade, and New Holland 7308 loader.
