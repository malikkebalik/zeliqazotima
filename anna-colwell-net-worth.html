Anna Colwell is an actress whose career started in the late 2000s. She debuted in an episode of Drop Dead Diva. In 2010, she had her first film role in Killers. She later made appearances on the TV shows Single Ladies, Necessary Roughness, and One Tree Hill. At the same time, she landed some roles in the movies The Change-Up, The Greening of Whitney Brown, and Journey 2: The Mysterious Island. In 2012, she had a recurring character as Brynn on The Game. That same year, she appeared on the miniseries Coma and on the TV film Steel Magnolias. She went on to have roles in the films Blended, Survivor’s Remorse, Furious 7, Term Life, and Burden.
