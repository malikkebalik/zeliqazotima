The 'Are You The One' Season 5 Cast Is Ready To Take A Chance For Love

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  8 minutes  |  1512 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

This winter is about to heat up with the Season 5 premiere of Are You the One? on Wednesday, Jan. 11 at 9 p.m. ET. The bold love experiment is back and better than ever, and there's also going to be a new twist this time around. So who's up to the task in finding love in one of the most unique dating shows to ever hit TV? The Are You the One? Season 5 cast is full of fierce competitors looking for their perfect match.

If you've heard of Are You the One?, you probably know that its part-dating show, part-reality competition. The basic premise of the series is that MTV casts a bunch of singles who go through a rigorous matchmaking process and an algorithm to find each cast member's "perfect match" among the bunch. If they find their so-called "perfect match" by the end of the season, the couple will split a cash prize of $1 million. Not to mention, the contestants supposedly found true love, so all in all, this sounds like a pretty sweet deal.

But what about that aforementioned new twist, you ask? Well, Are You the One? is adding an additional couple to the mix this season, which means that instead of the usual 10 couples, there will be 11 in this installment. Though that may seem like an insignificant change to the game, it'll actually make it more difficult for the contestants to find "the one," especially with the same 10 tries to find him or her.

So who are the brave souls willing to take on this whole new challenge? Get to know the contestants looking for love — and some money — this season of Are You the One?.
